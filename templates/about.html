Our History

A Legacy of Passion

Founded by the Prela family, our company began as a small boutique garage in Sursee with a deep passion for precision engineering and luxury automobiles. Over the decades, we have grown into Prela Automobile AG, a cornerstone of the Swiss automotive market.

The owners, with their extensive background in mechanical engineering and international trade, have always prioritized personal relationships with clients. Today, that same family-driven passion continues to guide our operations, ensuring that Prela Automobile AG remains a name synonymous with trust and quality.

We are uniquely positioned because we have years of experience in reputable car repairs and auto handling, a legacy of technical expertise that sets us apart in the Swiss market.
