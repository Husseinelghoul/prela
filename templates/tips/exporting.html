What to look for when exporting a car

Exporting a vehicle from Switzerland involves customs and logistics expertise. Here is how to navigate the process:

  • EUR.1 Document: This document is crucial for preferential customs treatment in many countries.
  • VAT Refund: If you are exporting a vehicle for commercial use, you may be eligible for a VAT refund.
  • Export Plates: You will need temporary export plates (Zollschilder) if you plan to drive the car out of the country.
  • Logistics Partner: Choose a partner with experience in container or RoRo shipping.
