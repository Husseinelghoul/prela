What to look for when selling a car

Selling your car in Switzerland requires careful preparation to ensure you get the best value and comply with legal requirements. Here are our top tips:

  • Complete Documentation: Ensure you have the service book (Serviceheft) and the vehicle registration document (Fahrzeugausweis).
  • Clean & Professional: A professional cleaning can increase the resale value significantly.
  • Recent MFK: A recent "Motorfahrzeugkontrolle" (MFK) makes your car much more attractive to private buyers.
  • Fair Pricing: Use market tools to set a competitive price based on mileage and condition.
